Ahmed Abo ElHaz

Ahmed Abu Elhaz is the Founder and CEO of Shezlong. An Affordable, anonymous online psychotherapy platform with professional, licensed therapists.

He has previously been the Marketing Director and Business Development Manager at Nasher Media. Ahmed earned himself an experience in marketing, having previously worked as the Digital Marketing Director at Hammock Tours and Marketing Specialist at Golden Thread.

He graduated from the Faculty of Engineering, Mansoura University, with a bachelor degree in Computers & Systems.

Ahmed Rizk

Ahmed Rizk is the Education Manager at Startup School. He is responsible for analysis curriculum design and development, along with delivery and feedback.

Rizk has founded SEED (Social Entrepreneurship Empowering Development), a project empowering Social Entrepreneurs in 2012. He has been the Business Development Manager then the CEO for the Swiss Training Institute in Egypt.

He has been a trainer since 2008, with double certification in Training; specializing in Adult Learning and Facilitation.

Rizk previously worked with multinationals like P&G, Vodafone Egypt and Teleperformance Egypt. He also worked with National Companies like MDS (Master Dental Society) and several NGOs as well, including UNICEF Egypt, Fard Foundation, Alhassan Foundation for Spinal Cord Injuries and Resala. He’s been working at Injaz as a part-time trainer for 2 years now.

Mohamed ElDib

Mohamed El Dib is the Co-Founder of Monkeys in Tuxedos; a creative studio in Cairo. He had previously worked in a lot of fields, he was the Chief Executive Officer of a restaurant; The Colour Green.

He Co-founded disalata.com, of which he was also the Marketing and Branding Guru.

He was Accounts Executive of Inspire Consulting Group. In addition to being the Chief Executive Officer of Entrepreneur’s Society.

Mohamed was born in the UK, he graduated from the American University in Cairo with a Bachelor of Business Administration (B.B.A), Marketing.

Mohamed Gamal

Mohamed Gamal is the founder of Kotobna, the first online publishing platform in Egypt. Kotobna is the winner of MIT Competition 2015, the biggest startups competition in Middle east. It’s also the winner of SeedStars competition 2015 and TDF Business Planning competition 2007 organized by UNDB.

Gamal earned more than 11 years of experience working in Marketing and Business Development inside IT industry. And he played a significant role in promoting many startups like Al-Khawarzmi, IPLMedia and Wasa.

Sabrine Assem

Sabrine Assem is the Co-Founder and CEO of Untap technologies, an innovation agency in Cairo working with organizations to plan innovation seeking campaigns that involve employees, partners, startups, customers, and the crowd at large. She previously founded SolverMine, and worked as an Entrepreneurship Consultant & Researcher at German-Arab Chamber of Industry and Commerce. She also worked as a Management Consultant at Nutty Scientists Egypt.

Sabrine graduated from The German University of Cairo with a bachelor degree in Science, Innovation Management, and Information System.
