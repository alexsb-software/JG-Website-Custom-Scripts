Ahmed El Dabaa

Ahmad El Dabaa has a broad experience in PR/FR field in different civil community organizations, as well as projects’ management skills in corporate world.

Dabaa is currently working as a "Projects Manager" for Procter & Gamble Egypt. Before that, he Co-founded Spark Egypt Training Agency. He served as the head of the Public Relations Committee at IEEE Alexandria Student Branch. In 2010 he Co-Founded Step-Up youth initiative, and served as its first HR head and as a Steering Committee member. Ahmad is involved in conducting various training sessions for Personal Development as Soft Skills Trainer.

Mahmoud Mansi

Mahmoud Mansi is a 4 times TEDx speaker, social entrepreneur, award-winning author, creative writing instructor, business journalist and a photographer. He has attended several international HR conferences, he was a speaker in around 50 seminars, and yet Mahmoud keeps the balance and remains an employee as a Senior HR Specialist & an HR Lecturer teaching in the MBA program in the AASTMT University in Egypt.

In addition to being the Co-Founder of The AlexandErian Magazine and the Founder of HR Revolution Middle East Magazine. He believes that HR cannot only change the organization, but it can change the world. He is the Founder of “Shared Development” HR Model, and is currently writing a new book called: “HR for Millennials”.

Mansi believes in the importance of being a follower and a leader at the same time, and just recently in 2017, he was the Chairperson of Rome International Careers Course in Italy, the Chairperson of HR Champions Conference in Cairo, and the Chairperson of Entrepreneurship OverScene Summit in the Bibliotheca Alexandrina.

Aya Eldighady

Aya Eldighady is a Certified Career Counselor & GCDF, who was born in Alexandria and started to study psychology at Alexandria University in 2005. She was accredited from Canadian Center for Education at 2010 as a career counselor, to be one of the very first to earn this accreditation in Egypt and the Arab world.

She worked for several organizations as a freelance career counselor. She is also a member of the team that initiated the first career development center in 10 governorates across Egypt. In 2012 she joined AM Foundation for career development and initiated a research and development team that implemented many projects to promote the culture of career development in Egypt.

In 2014 Aya co-founded SMART Careers to carry on the mission of developing educational programs to enhance the formal education system and promote the future.
